Our Story

The Story

The Table Bianchi was founded with a simple idea: hospitality should go beyond catering.

The name reflects the heritage of Chef Guilherme "Gui" Bianchi, whose family roots come from Northern Italy — a place where food, family and the table are deeply connected.

Chef Gui grew up surrounded by the traditions of Italian cooking, where meals are about bringing people together and creating memorable moments.

Over the years, he built his career working in catering, private dining and corporate events, gaining experience in different countries before establishing The Table Bianchi in London.

His approach is simple: great food, carefully prepared, served with professionalism and attention to every detail.

This philosophy is at the heart of The Table Bianchi.

A História

A The Table Bianchi foi fundada com uma ideia simples: hospitalidade deve ir além do catering.

O nome reflete a herança do Chef Guilherme "Gui" Bianchi, cujas raízes familiares vêm do Norte da Itália — um lugar onde comida, família e a mesa estão profundamente conectados.

Chef Gui cresceu cercado pelas tradições da culinária italiana, onde as refeições são sobre reunir pessoas e criar momentos memoráveis.
